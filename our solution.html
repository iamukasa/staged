Sanitary Pad distribution services

We distribute a single packed sanitary pad through Ari. Ari is our sanitary pad dispenser that offers women and girls the option to purchase pads when they want depending on need and financial situation. Traditional packaging and distribution channels of sanitary pads do not reach women and girls where they are and an entire pack is usually expensive. Our machines allows for flexible and accessible purchase of pads right in the women's and girl's washrooms. " One simply inserts a 10 shilling coin in the coin slot. This will trigger the machine to drop one pad where then you can use it as regularly. We are having a campaign is selected locations where for every pad sold. The proceedings will go into creating better access to a girl in need.

Wazi By Ari

Sexual Reproductive Health Intiative

We are not just a sanitary pad distribution company. Every time we went out to teach young girls how to use the machines we realized that there was an information gap on Menstrual Education and as a whole Sexual Reproductive Education. That is when Wazi was born. Wazi is a Kiswahili word which means open. We aim to demystify myths concerning sexual reproduction in particular Menstruation. We want young girls to be equiped to handle the transition to womanhood without viewing it as a burden Womanhood is a gift, something to be celebrated not to be seen as a curse - Munira Twahir, Founder of Inteco Kenya. We focus on 6 topics namely: Reproduction organs Menstruation Sex Risks involved: physically, emotionally and financially Contraception Relationships. We split these session into 3 hour sessions in 10 groups for 2 weeks in an academic term. Thereafter we carry out an impact assessment in the following term.
